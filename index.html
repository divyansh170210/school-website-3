Campus Life

School Church

Was build in 1978

This church was established by our Rev. Father John. We all go to the church after assembly for 2 minutes meditation. We all pray for our day to be well.



School Library

Was build in 1985

This Library was established by Sr. Rupheena. We all go to library every week in the break time to read some books. Me and my friends love to read books.



School Auditorium

Was build in 1990

This Auditorium was established by Rev. Father Paul. We all celebrate all the functions in the auditorium. Like on Republic Day, Christmas, Independence Day etc. for the acts.

School Achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution

Our school won 1st prize in Inter School Debate Competition on environment pollution. we all were so proud. our pair won and we all celebrated our win. and we went on a holiday to celebrate the win.

Inter School Football Competition
Came first in Inter School Football Competition

Our school won gold medal in Inter School Football Competition. We all were so excited to see our football team play. In the first match we were defeated but after that we made a comeback and won 3 consecutive matches and we were in the semi finals. In semi finals we won a one sided match by 3-0. In finals we made a last killer in the 91st minute of the match and we won by 2-1 and we won the tournament.

Inter School Science Competition
Won second prize in inter school Science Competition

our school came 2nd in inter school Science Competition. we all thought we could come 1st but the other school was little bit ahead in the projects. but still we all awarded our science team with silver medals and we liked our school's projects.
